Our Clinic Services

IVF-ICSI

VF & ICSI is a highly effective fertility technique where the egg and sperm...

VF & ICSI is a highly effective fertility technique where the egg and sperm are allowed to fertilize in a lab. Once the embryo is formed. it is transferred into the uterus, possibly helping you conceive a baby.

IUI (Intrauterine Insemination)

IUI is a simpler and less invasive treatment option...

IUI is a simpler and less invasive treatment option that directly places sperm into a woman’s uterus for fertilization to take place.

Male Infertility Treatment

Male infertility can also lead to an inability in getting...

Male infertility can also lead to an inability in getting a fertile female pregnant and conceiving a baby. We offer procedures like Sperm Retrieval for male infertility treatment.

Sperm Washing and Semen Processing

Sperm washing is a process to remove any mucus or...

Sperm washing is a process to remove any mucus or immobile sperm in the semen for increasing the chances of fertilization.

FET

If your current cycle is unsuccessful or you decide later that you would like to try...

If your current cycle is unsuccessful or you decide later that you would like to try for another baby, the frozen embryos can be thawed and transferred without you having to undergo another egg collection procedure.

Blastocyst Transfer

A blastocyst transfer is an embryo transfer which involves transferring one or more...

A blastocyst transfer is an embryo transfer which involves transferring one or more embryos that are at a very advanced stage of development.

Its goal is to provide regular check-ups that allow doctors or midwives to treat and prevent potential health problems throughout the course of the pregnancy.
